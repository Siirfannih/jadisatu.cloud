# Jadisatu — Product Catalog

## AI Agent Sales Conversion (Produk Utama)

AI yang bales chat customer 24/7, gak pernah cuti, gak pernah bad mood.
Terima beres — klien gak perlu ngerti AI.

### Paket

| Paket | Harga/bulan | Channel | Chat Limit | Fitur |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | Rp 1.500.000 | 1 (WA) | 500 | AI 24/7, setup, dashboard basic |
| Growth | Rp 3.000.000 | 2 (WA + IG) | 2000 | + Lead scoring, analytics, priority support |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Unlimited | + Custom integration, SLA, dedicated AM |

### Yang Termasuk di Semua Paket
- Setup & training AI (terima beres)
- Customization tone & personality per bisnis
- Knowledge base setup (produk, FAQ, pricing)
- Dashboard monitoring
- Human handoff mechanism
- Monthly performance report

### Add-ons
- Extra channel: +Rp 500.000/channel/bulan
- Extra chat volume: +Rp 500.000/1000 chat
- Custom integration: mulai dari Rp 2.000.000 (one-time)

## Value Proposition

| Aspek | Tanpa Jadisatu | Dengan Jadisatu |
|---|---|---|
| Jam kerja | 8-10 jam/hari | 24/7 |
| Cost | Rp 3-5jt/admin/bulan | Mulai Rp 1.5jt |
| Response time | 5-30 menit | 3-15 detik |
| Consistency | Tergantung mood admin | Selalu konsisten |
| Data | Di kepala admin | Tersimpan otomatis |
| Scalability | 1 admin = ~50 chat/hari | Unlimited |

## Target Market

- UMKM Indonesia dengan bisnis online
- Punya volume chat > 30/hari
- Jualan via WhatsApp / Instagram
- Kewalahan handle customer service
- Budget Rp 1.5 - 5jt/bulan untuk operasional
